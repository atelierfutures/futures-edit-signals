      Methodology & notes

      We track signals across fashion, beauty, and wellness articles and tag each one with a primary keyword, status (emerging/bubbling/mainstream), and category. Filters set your current window. For momentum, we compare against a same-length previous window (e.g., if you pick last 90 days, we also look at the 90 days before that).

      Inspired by research workflows (scraping, normalizing, and clustering), we emphasize editorially useful views: volume (Top keywords), distribution (Status mix), source balance, momentum (Fastest-rising), and first appearances (Newcomers). Network clustering is out of scope here but could be added later if we enrich each article with multiple keywords.
